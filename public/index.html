Why does a ruler need calibrating?

A screen only knows its resolution (e.g. 1920×1080), not its physical size — a 24″ monitor and a 6″ phone can share the same resolution. Browsers assume a fixed 96 pixels per inch, which is only right for some displays. Matching a card whose size is standardized worldwide (ISO 7810 ID-1) pins the ruler to real millimetres.
